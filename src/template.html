About me

My first creative love was fashion, I have designed for companies such as Calvin Klein, Talbots, Cache and GIII Apparel.  After 10 years in the fashion world, I merged my interests of customer centered products and digital design to start my journey in UX Design.  

While having a similar design process, I felt it was an easy transition while obtaining a UX Design Certificate at New York University.  I'm passionate with understanding user's and finding the best solutions possible.  I'm eager to both build on my academic foundations in user centric design and stay in tune with the latest digital design strategies.
